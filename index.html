Two Variable Linear Equations

Problem 1

x + 2y = 7

2x - y = 4

Step 1: Solve the first equation for x:

x = 7 - 2y

Step 2: Substitute x into the second equation:

2(7 - 2y) - y = 4

14 - 4y - y = 4

-5y = -10

y = 2

Step 3: Substitute y back into the first equation:

x + 2(2) = 7

x + 4 = 7

x = 3

Solution: x = 3, y = 2

Problem 2

2x + 3y = 35

5x + 3y = 65

Step 1: Subtract the first equation from the second:

(5x + 3y) - (2x + 3y) = 65 - 35

3x = 30

x = 10

Step 2: Substitute x back into the first equation:

2(10) + 3y = 35

20 + 3y = 35

3y = 15

y = 5

Solution: x = 10, y = 5

Problem 3

4x - y = 11

3x + 2y = 18

Step 1: Solve the first equation for y:

y = 4x - 11

Step 2: Substitute y into the second equation:

3x + 2(4x - 11) = 18

3x + 8x - 22 = 18

11x - 22 = 18

11x = 40

x = 40/11

Step 3: Substitute x back into the first equation:

4(40/11) - y = 11

160/11 - y = 11

-y = 11 - 160/11

-y = (121 - 160) / 11

y = 39/11

Solution: x = 40/11, y = 39/11

Problem 4

2x + y = 10

3x - y = 5

Step 1: Add the two equations:

(2x + y) + (3x - y) = 10 + 5

5x = 15

x = 3

Step 2: Substitute x back into the first equation:

2(3) + y = 10

6 + y = 10

y = 4

Solution: x = 3, y = 4

Problem 5

4x + 2y = 14

3x - y = 5

Step 1: Solve the second equation for y:

y = 3x - 5

Step 2: Substitute y into the first equation:

4x + 2(3x - 5) = 14

4x + 6x - 10 = 14

10x - 10 = 14

10x = 24

x = 24 / 10
